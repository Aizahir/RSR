about me

 Silkiie, D. Rene'e Harris started studying piano at
3 years old, first song Pennies from Heaven that was taught to her by a couple that watched over her and her younger sister. Her father was a drummer and mother a pianist playing together until his jealousy was overwhelming and dangerous for their mother who left and continued to play with well known musicians; toured with Earl Bostic. Continuing to study music, branched into Dancing Burlesque and where she picked up the name "Silkiie.". . When she met back with her mother after a long personal search and paying an investigator to locate her mother, she continued to call her Silkiie until the last day they talked and she said, she apologized to her and called her by her first name.

Continuing to study and play several instruments, piano became her passion just like her mother's passion playing in clubs and sitting in on open night Mike and later creating her own band in 1999 named Rhythm Spirit and later renamed Rhythm Spirit Rhythm.....
